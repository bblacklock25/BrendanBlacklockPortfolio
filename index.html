This design employs a Basys 3 FPGA Board to control a Rover 5. The rover avoids wall, detects the colors red, blue, and green, and receives morse-code commands. For this project, I coded the Verilog modules and testbenches for wall avoidance and morse-code communication. I also designed and tested circuits for overcurrent protection, wall avoidance, and morse-code communication.

This design utilizes a Raspberry Pi 4 to automate a catapult launch. The camera determines target position. The target distance and height are calculated; launch angle and force are determined using these values. Launch angle is adjusted and the projectile is fired at the calculated force. After launch, the projectile position is found. If not hit, the catapult is recalibrated until 2 hits in a row are registred.
